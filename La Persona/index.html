LA Persona


The colosseum presents you a once in a lifetime chance to flaunt your greatest weapon, your personality. Do not let it slip, for you will be lost. Your confidence and your demeanour will be forced into the limelight and judged. Your persona will be your boon or bane. Welcome to LA Persona.



Rules:

1. One team can represent each school.
2. The teams will be assigned a topic just before the event.
3. The team is expected to prepare three sets of costumes relevant to the topic.
4. Materials like newspapers, paints, staples, tape, tissues, glue, magazines and cloth in their designs will be provided.
5. You may choose music during your 2-minute ramp walk. The designers are anticipated to receive questions from the judges while the models go down the runway.
6. Over your respective school uniforms, you should wear your designed costumes.

Judging Criteria:



1:There will be 4 rounds:1 written preliminary round and 3 on-stage rounds.
   R1: Introduction round: Introduce yourself in an amusing way.
   R2: Prop Round: Use a prop and present it to the audience.
   R3: Survival Round: Enact a situation using your wit and humour.
2:Participants are supposed to be dressed decently.
3:No vulgarity or obscenity will be tolerated.
Judgement Criteria

1. Spontaneity
2. Humour
3. Stage Presence
4. Creativity
5. Confidence
6. Linguistic Competence
