LA Persona


The Colosseum presents you a once-in-a-lifetime chance to exhibit your greatest weapon, your personality. Do not let it slip, for you will be lost. Your confidence and your demeanour will be forced into the limelight and judged. Your persona will be your boon or bane. Welcome to LA Persona.



Rules:

1. There will be four rounds: One written preliminary round and three onstage rounds.
  R1. Introduction round: Introduce yourself in an amusing way.
  R2. Prop Round: Use a prop and present it to the audience.
  R3. Survival Round: Enact a situation using your wit and humour.

2. Participants are supposed to be dressed decently.

3. No vulgarity or obscenity will be tolerated.



Judging Criteria:


1. Spontaneity

2. Humour

3. Stage Presence

4. Creativity

5. Confidence

6. Linguistic Competence
