LA Persona


The Colosseum presents you a once-in-a-lifetime chance to exhibit your greatest asset, your personality. Do not let it slip, for you will be lost. Your confidence and your demeanour will be forced into the limelight and judged. Your persona will be your boon or bane. Welcome to LA Persona.



This personality event has always been the premier event of LA fest. It will put your persona through rigorous trials. This event comes with a promise: youâ€™ll come out of it stronger than before.

Rules:

1. A school can send a maximum of four participants.

2. There will be five rounds: Two preliminary rounds, one written and one surprise round.

  R1: Introduction round: Introduce yourself in an amusing way.
  R2: Situation Round: The participants will be given situations and their reactions will be judged.
  R3: Judges Round:Judges round: The participants will be questioned by the judges.

3. Participants are expected to be dressed modestly.

4. No vulgarity or obscenity will be tolerated.



Judging Criteria:


1. Spontaneity

2. Humour

3. Stage presence

4. Creativity

5. Confidence

6. Linguistic competence
