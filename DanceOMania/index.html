Rules:

1: Only 8-16 participants are allowed from each school.
2: Performance should not exceed 8 min.
3: Choose any music or track.
4: Party poppers or any such explosive items are not allowed.
5: All sequences must be performed on stage.
6: Obscenity or vulgarity(in the songs or actions during performances) is not allowed. 7: Backup CDs or USBs can be brought by the contestants.
8: Special stage settings are allowed but with prior arrangements with the event heads.
9: Special lighting may not be available. Depend upon such lighting for your dance sequence only If it can be brought by the respective teams.
