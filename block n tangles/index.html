Block N Tangles

“You can be true to your own character or be a true actor.”


The Colosseum gives you a chance to weave yourselves into the very fabric of your characters and animate them immaculately. Unfurl your wings and command the skies. Block N’ Tangles await.

Teamwork makes the dream work. Work together with your teammates, and ensure your dream doesn't become a nightmare in this improv event, where you will need to think on the spot, adjust to your partners’ dialogues and create a story from nothing.



Rules:

1. There will be two offstage preliminary rounds, one written preliminary round for which each school can send two three-member teams.

2. Only eight teams will move to the second preliminary round which is a group discussion round.

3. Only four teams will qualify for the final onstage event.

4. Onstage event will have three rounds.


Judging Criteria:


1. Spontaneous wit and humour

2. Creativity

3. Acting skills

4. Coordination as a group
