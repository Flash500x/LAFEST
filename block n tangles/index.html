Block N Tangles

“If you can be true to your own character you are a true actor.”


The colosseum gives you a chance to thread yourselves into the very fabric of your characters and animate them immaculately. Unfurl your wings and command the skies. Block N’ Tangles await.



Rules:

1. A school can send any number of three-member teams for the written preliminary round.

2. Only eight teams move to the semi finals.

3. Only four teams will qualify for the final event.

4. Onstage event will have three rounds.




Judging Criteria:


1. Spontaneous wit and humour

2. Creativity

3. Acting skills

4. Coordination as a group
