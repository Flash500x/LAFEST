Enquesta

Back to Events

Observe the world around you for we will ask you questions about things you failed to notice. Keep your minds sharp and may Athena stand behind you in this war of wisdom and knowledge. Use all your senses to extrapolate from the subtle clues in the questionnaire and emerge victorious in this Colosseum.




Rules:



1. No team limit for a school in the preliminary round

2. Two members per team.

3. There will be a preliminary round with twenty questions to select two teams for the Finals.

4. There can be no more than two teams from the same school in the Finals.

5. The Finals will consist of six rounds out of which there will be eliminations in the third and the fifth rounds.

6. The questions can be of Pounce and Bounce, Buzzer or Point Auction format.

7. The Quizmaster's decision is final.
