Observe the world around you for we will ask you the questions about things you failed to notice.
Keep your minds sharp and may Athena stand behind you in this war of wisdom and knowledge.
Use all your senses to extrapolate from the subtle clues in the questionnaire and emerge victorious in this Colosseum.




Rules:



1. No team limit for a school in the quiz.

2. 2 people per team.

3. There will be a preliminary round with 20 questions to select 6 teams for the Finals .

4. There can be no more than 2 teams from the same school in the Finals.

5. The Finals will consist of 6 rounds out of which there will be eliminations in the 3rd and the 5th rounds.

6. The questions can be of Pounce and Bounce, Buzzer or Point Auction format.

7. The Quizmaster's decision is final.
