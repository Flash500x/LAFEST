Harmony

Back to Events

The Muses await in the Colosseum to meet their equals. Enchant the crowd with your musical prowess. Modulate your pitch, notes and chords to bewitch and sway the audience with your performances. Break away from the humdrum of everyday life; stand apart and dance to your own music.


Rules:

1. A minimum of six and a maximum of twelve participants are allowed in a team.

2. Six output cables, five mics, two keyboard stands and a standard drum kit will be provided.

3. There is only one round with songs in any language and an original remake of a popular song.

4. Each participating team has twelve minutes for performance and two minutes for stage setting.

5. A minimum of three instruments and a maximum of eight instruments are required, including the drums which will be provided. Each team can have a maximum of four vocalists.

6. Devotional songs and classical songs are not allowed.

7. Additional instruments and accessories should be brought by the contestants if required. Amplifiers will be made available to teams upon request.

8. The use of rhythm boxes, pre-recorded MIDI files (other than those used as per the rule below), karaoke, drum pads and rhythm pads is not permitted.

9. USB-MIDI controllers, synthesisers and shoulder synthesisers are permitted on condition that they are pre-recorded MIDI loops.


Judging Criteria:


1. Vocal Quality

2. Coordination

3. Creativity

4. Rhythmic Interpretation
