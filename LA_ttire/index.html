L'attire

"Elegance is the only beauty that never fades."


We offer you the brainchild of the Batch of ‘23. This all-out fashion show that is unique to this edition of LA Fest, focused on the originality of the participants and the elegance of their designs. Conquer the stage and mesmerise the audience with your scintillating designs and exquisite taste in fashion.



Rules:

1. One team can represent each school.

2. Teams will be assigned a topic three hours before the event.

3. Each team is expected to prepare three sets of costumes relevant to the topic.

4. All teams are expected to bring the materials required to design the costumes.

5. Newspaper, cardboard, sack, chart paper, crepe paper, stationery and other biodegradable materials are allowed. Use of plastic in your designs is prohibited.

6. The music of your choice may be played during the two-minute ramp walk. The designers are expected to answer the judges’ questions while the models go down the runway.

7. The designed costume must be worn over the school uniform of the participants.



Judging Criteria:


1. Presentation and Design

2. Creativity

3. Skill in handling the theme

4. Confidence and attitude of the models

5. Elegance
