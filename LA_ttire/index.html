L'attire

"Elegance is the only beauty that never fades."


A unique addition to this edition of La Fest, and all-out fashion show "L'Attire", focusing on the creativity of the participants and the elegance of their designs. Conquer the stage and mesmerise the audience with your scintillating designs and exquisite taste in fashion.



This fashion event is the coinage of the batch of 2023. We aspire to find prospective designers and models within the multitude of participants appearing for La Fest. Exhibit your fashion prowess in this event.

Rules:

1. Only one team of three models and three designers can represent a school.

2. Teams will be assigned a theme a day before the event.

3. Each team will be given five hours to design the attire.

4. Each team is expected to prepare three sets of costumes relevant to the theme.

5. Costumes are to the designed using only materials provided by the host school.

6. You may play music of your choice during the two-minute ramp walk. The designers are expected to answer the judgesâ€™ questions during the Questionnaire round.

There will be two rounds:

Rampwalk:  Walk down the stage and show off your fashionable side as well as your modelling skills.

Questionnaire:  Answer the questions posed to you confidently and show the judges your intellectual side.

7. The designed costume must be worn over any other attire like black kurta/kurti and trousers.


Judging Criteria:


1. Presentation and design

2. Creativity

3. Skill in handling the theme

4. Confidence and attitude of the models

5. Elegance
