L'attire

"Elegance is the only beauty that never fades."


A unique addition to this edition of La Fest, and all-out fashion show "L'Attire", focusing on the creativity of the participants and the elegance of their designs. Conquer the stage and mesmerise the audience with your scintillating designs and exquisite taste in fashion.



This fashion event is the brainchild of the batch of 2023. We aspire to find prospective designers and models within the multitude of participants appearing for La Fest. Exhibit your fashion prowess in this event.

Rules:

1. Only one team of three models and three designers can represent a school.

2. Teams will be assigned a theme four hours before the event.

3. Each team is expected to prepare three sets of costumes relevant to the theme.

4. Costumes are to the designed using only materials provided by the host school.

5. You may play music of your choice during the two-minute ramp walk. The designers are expected to answer the judgesâ€™ questions while the models go down the ramp.

6. The designed costume must be worn over any other attire like kurta/kurti and trousers.



Judging Criteria:


1. Presentation and design

2. Creativity

3. Skill in handling the theme

4. Confidence and attitude of the models

5. Elegance
