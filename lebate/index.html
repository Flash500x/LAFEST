L'ebate

Tune into your inner scholars, and unleash all your knowledge in this battle of the words. Leave no fact unchecked as you try to pin down your opponents with your well-built opinions. Use all your strength, and you might just beat them all in this debate event.


Rules:

1. Two participants per team.

2. One offstage preliminary round will determine the four teams that would qualify for the Finals.

3. Prelims Rules: Speakers will be allowed two minutes to deliver their points, after which the opposition can ask questions for four minutes maximum. A maximum of three questions are allowed.

4. Finals rules: The proposing team will be given five minutes to deliver their points, after which the opposition teams can ask a maximum of three questions.

5. Participants will not be allowed to change their stand during the course of the debate.

6. No new arguments may be introduced in the rebuttal period. During the rebuttal session, the speaker is not allowed to seek the help of his teammate.

7. In the questioning period, the questioner may ask any fair, clear question that has a direct bearing on the debate. Cross questioning is not allowed.

8. Any gains made outside of the established procedure are disallowed.



Judging Criteria:


1. Analysis

2. Evidence and reasoning

3. Organization

4. Delivery (Voice, language and deportment)

5. Command of language

6. Adherence to the time limit
