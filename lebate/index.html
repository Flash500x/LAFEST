L'ebate

Tune into your inner scholars, and unleash all your worldly knowledge in this battle of the words. Leave no fact unchecked as you try to pin down your opponents with your well-built opinions. Use all your strength, and you might just beat them all in this debate event.


Rules:

1: two participants per team
2: two preliminary rounds (offstage) will determine the 4 teams that qualify to the on-stage rounds
3: The on-stage round will determine the 2 teams that qualify to the Finals.
4: Prelims Rules: Speakers will be allowed 2 min to deliver their points, after which the opposition can ask questions for 3 minutes [maximum of 3 questions].
5: On-stage Rules: The Speaking Team will be given 5 min to deliver their points, after which the opposition can ask questions for 3 minutes [maximum of 3 questions].
6: Participants will not be allowed to change their stand during the course of the debate.
7: No new constructive arguments may be introduced in the rebuttal period. The affirmative must, if possible, reply to the major negative arguments before the last rebuttal. During the rebuttal session, the speaker is not allowed to seek the help of his teammate.
8: In the questioning period, the questioner may ask any fair, clear question that has a direct bearing on the debate. Cross questioning is not allowed.
9: Any gains made outside of the established procedure are disallowed.



Judging Criteria:


1. Literary Skills
2. Language Proficiency
3. Confidence/Poise
4. Clarity of Arguments
5. Adherence to the time limit
6. Valid Proof
